How It Works
Checkout happens while you shop.

LineSkip uses real-time computer vision to detect and log items as they go into your cart. By the time you're done shopping, you're already checked out.

1

Grab a Cart

The LineSkip unit is already mounted and ready. No app download, no account needed.

2

Shop Normally

Drop items into the cart. The camera instantly recognizes each product using YOLOv8 computer vision.

3

Review Your Cart

The 7" touchscreen shows your running total and itemized list in real time.

4

Pay & Go

Pay right on the screen. Walk out. No checkout line, no cashier, no wait.

Why LineSkip
Built different. Priced right.

Every smart cart on the market costs a fortune. LineSkip was designed from day one to be deployable at scale.

💰

Radically Affordable

At ~$359/cart, LineSkip is 15–30× cheaper than competitors like Caper Cart ($5K–$10K) and Amazon Dash Cart.

📷

Vision-Only Architecture

No weight sensors, no RFID, no complex hardware. Just a camera, a Raspberry Pi 5, and a trained model.

⚡

On-Device Inference

Everything runs locally on the Pi. No cloud dependency, no latency, no subscription to an AI API.

🏪

Built for Grocery

Designed specifically for grocery retail. Easy to mount, easy to maintain, and easy to scale across a store fleet.

🛒

Zero Friction UX

Shoppers don't need an app, a loyalty card, or any tech knowledge. It just works — right in the cart.
